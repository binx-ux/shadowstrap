# Shadowstrap Community Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want Shadowstrap to be a welcoming community for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, or identity. Everyone who participates in this project, through issues, pull requests, Discord, or any other channel, is expected to follow this code of conduct.

## What We Expect

**Be respectful.** Disagreements happen. Criticize ideas, not people. No insults, harassment, or personal attacks — ever.

**Be constructive.** If you report a bug, include steps to reproduce it. If you request a feature, explain why it's useful. Vague or dismissive contributions don't help anyone.

**Be honest.** Don't claim others' work as your own. Don't spread misinformation about the project, its safety, or its maintainers.

**Stay on topic.** Issues and pull requests are for project-related discussion. Keep conversations relevant.

**No spam.** Don't post the same issue or message multiple times. Don't promote unrelated projects or services.

## What We Don't Allow

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation of any kind
- Discriminatory language or behaviour based on age, ethnicity, gender, nationality, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or any other personal characteristic
- Sharing private information about others without their explicit consent
- Deliberately misleading users about where to download Shadowstrap or its safety
- Encouraging or facilitating violations of Roblox's Terms of Service beyond what Shadowstrap already does by design
- Distributing malware or malicious modifications under the Shadowstrap name

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

1. A warning from a maintainer
2. Temporary removal from community spaces (Discord, GitHub discussions)
3. Permanent ban for repeated or severe violations

Maintainers have the final say on what constitutes a violation and what the appropriate response is.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation, please report it by:

- Opening a private issue tagged `[conduct]`, **or**
- Messaging a moderator on the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/nKjV3mGq6R)

Reports will be handled confidentially. We will not publicly identify reporters without their permission.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all Shadowstrap spaces — GitHub (issues, pull requests, discussions), Discord, and any other community platform operated by the project maintainers.
